CASSANDRA BASSO

CAKE CONNOISSEUR

DANIELLE WILLIAMSON

INNOVATION OFFICER
Danielle started baking cake as a way to knit her loves of food and science into one. Any bit of her spare time is spent playing with new combinations and recipes.
